The Cat-Burrito is coming

A brand new innovative 3D collectathon platformer: "Cat-Burrito: The Quest for Margaritas" is currenly in development at Darling Games. The game will both remind the player of past games from the Nintendo 64 and Playstation 1 era while also offering some new and interesting elements and mechanics. The game will be a short but sweet experience to be released on Steam and itch.io.
