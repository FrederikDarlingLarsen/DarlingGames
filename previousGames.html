Lumberjack Samurai (2022)

This game was created at the Nordic Game Jam 2022. The theme was "theme garden" meaning that everyone had differnt themes determined by paper flowers that had to be chosen. The themes were divided into categories by the color of the flowers. We decided to pick the category "mechanic" and picked the theme "cutting". This led to an idea about a samurai cutting trees.

In the game it is all about cutting trees to gain exp and get to level 4 where you will be able to cut down the Elden Tree. In level 1 the player can only cut down oak trees, then birch trees in level 2 and pine trees in level 3.

Since this game was made in less than 48 hours the gameplay is not that varied. I spent a lot of time getting the cutting of the meshes to work and the script for randomly generating most of the game elements. The game was made in a team of three people which all had designated roles. I was the Lead Programmer, and the other two were responsible for the assets and the UI.

Candy Shoot (2021)

This game was developed by me and a friend at a halloween game jam a AAU CPH in 2021. The aim of the game was simple to make a fun halloween inspired shooter with lots of candy. The game was placed third in the category of Fun to Play, wihch was also the category we were going for this time around.

In the game you wield a very cool halloween decorated deagle and you have to shoot appraching bats and spiders in the dark, foggy and scary forest. When the enemies are shot candy is spread out fron them and scattered everywhere, the player must then to to collect as much of the candy as possible while continung to shoot the enemies.

This game was done in less than 48 hours but i still managed to learn lot in process, i was responsible for all of the code and unity related stuff while my frind created the models. I learned about the unty nav meash system for the enies along with implementing smooth turning for them. A also worked with parts of the environment working with volumetric fog. A more complete verison of the game is planned for a steam release by haloween this year. It will be an exteneded version of the game jam game and it will be a free to play title.

Chad Game (2021)

This game was made at a small online game jam that functioned as a test for possible bigger game jams at AAU. I worked on this game with one of my firend and it was made in less than 48 hours.

The game focused a lot on story and text. The game centers around the Virgin who wants to be like Chad. He figures out through out the game that there are three things he needs to do. He needs to get an education, a place to live and a girlfriend. When he has achieved all of this he will win at life.

The game was not successful in accomplishing the most interesting gameplay, but i was a good learning process for the implementation of a basic text sytem and for at payer controller tat could move the player i 8 directions.

Turbo Cow (2018)

I attended a game jam at Vallekilde Højskole in Holbæk in 2018 with some friends. We did not realize unitl we got there that almost all attendants were professional game developers, which meant that we got to see the working progress of differnt indie game while we worked on our own game. This jam lasted a bit longer than the usual 48 hours, which means that we go to spend more time making a game that was much better than what we had done on other jams.

The game is a racing game, with procurally generated chunks of race track, where the player has to keep racing, endless runner style in order to best the high score. The idea was to create a very stylixed game utilixing the at the time relatively new HDRP(High Definition Render Pipeline) in Unity. We settled on a synthwave style game, with a soundtrack to match. One intersting feture that was added was to be able to play the game in synth maode or 8 bit mode. The would change the stlye of the music and a filter would be added to the screen in 8-bit to make it look like and old CRT monitor.

For this game I worked primarily on the sound fx an the music. I got to play around with LMMS for the music. We had some conversations about releasing the game on Steam, and chipped in a Steam direct page for the release. We never got to finish the game though, due to the three of us being busy with life stuff along with loosing interest in the project.
