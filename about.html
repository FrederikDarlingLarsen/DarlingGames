Hi, my name is Frederik Darling and I am the CEO Darling Games. I founded Darling Games with the aim of creating games that honor classic gaming traditions while still bringing something new to the table. The first published game from Darling Games: Cat-Burrito: The Quest for Margaritas will be a tribute to the collectathon platformers of the 90's while additionally adding some new and unique game mechanics.

This year (2023) I will be focusing on becoming a software developer to hopefully land a full-time job. This means that there will not be a lot of time for developing games. This means that "Cat-Burrito" will probably be delayed to 2024 with the ambition to release a demo sometime in 2023. I will hopefully also attend a couple of game jams this year, so some smaller games may be released this year as well.

Check out my portfolio using this site or take a look at my itch.io site or YouTube page to see what a have worked on over the years.
